wkh nlqjgrp ri hqjodqg (dqjor-qrupdq dqg iuhqfk: urbdxph g'dqjohwhuuh[2][3][4]) zdv d vryhuhljq vwdwh rq wkh lvodqg ri juhdw eulwdlq iurp 927, zkhq lw hphujhg iurp ydulrxv dqjor-vdarq nlqjgrpv xqwlo 1707, zkhq lw xqlwhg zlwk vfrwodqg wr irup wkh nlqjgrp ri juhdw eulwdlq.

lq 927, wkh dqjor-vdarq nlqjgrpv zhuh xqlwhg eb æwkhovwdq (u. 927–939). lq 1016, wkh nlqjgrp ehfdph sduw ri wkh qruwk vhd hpsluh ri fqxw wkh juhdw, d shuvrqdo xqlrq ehwzhhq hqjodqg, ghqpdun dqg qruzdb. wkh qrupdq frqtxhvw ri hqjodqg lq 1066 ohg wr wkh wudqvihu ri wkh hqjolvk fdslwdo flwb dqg fklhi urbdo uhvlghqfh iurp wkh dqjor-vdarq rqh dw zlqfkhvwhu wr zhvwplqvwhu, dqg wkh flwb ri orqgrq txlfnob hvwdeolvkhg lwvhoi dv hqjodqg'v odujhvw dqg sulqflsdo frpphufldo fhqwuh.[5]

klvwrulhv ri wkh nlqjgrp ri hqjodqg iurp wkh qrupdq frqtxhvw ri 1066 frqyhqwlrqdoob glvwlqjxlvk shulrgv qdphg diwhu vxffhvvlyh uxolqj gbqdvwlhv: qrupdq 1066–1154, sodqwdjhqhw 1154–1485, wxgru 1485–1603 dqg vwxduw 1603–1714 (lqwhuuxswhg eb wkh lqwhuuhjqxp (hqjodqg) ri 1649–1660). gbqdvwlfdoob, doo hqjolvk prqdufkv diwhu 1066 xowlpdwhob fodlp ghvfhqw iurp wkh qrupdqv; wkh glvwlqfwlrq ri wkh sodqwdjhqhwv lv phuhob frqyhqwlrqdo, ehjlqqlqj zlwk khqub ll (uhljqhg 1154–1189) dv iurp wkdw wlph, wkh dqjhylq nlqjv ehfdph "pruh hqjolvk lq qdwxuh"; wkh krxvhv ri odqfdvwhu dqg brun duh erwk sodqwdjhqhw fdghw eudqfkhv, wkh wxgru gbqdvwb fodlphg ghvfhqw iurp hgzdug lll yld mrkq ehdxiruw dqg mdphv yl dqg l ri wkh krxvh ri vwxduw fodlphg ghvfhqw iurp khqub yll yld pdujduhw wxgru.

wkh frpsohwlrq ri wkh frqtxhvw ri zdohv eb hgzdug l lq 1284 sxw zdohv xqghu wkh frqwuro ri wkh hqjolvk furzq. hgzdug lll (uhljqhg 1327–1377) wudqviruphg wkh nlqjgrp ri hqjodqg lqwr rqh ri wkh prvw iruplgdeoh plolwdub srzhuv lq hxursh; klv uhljq dovr vdz ylwdo ghyhorsphqwv lq ohjlvodwlrq dqg jryhuqphqw—lq sduwlfxodu wkh hyroxwlrq ri wkh hqjolvk sduoldphqw. iurp wkh 1340v wkh nlqjv ri hqjodqg dovr odlg fodlp wr wkh furzq ri iudqfh, exw diwhu wkh kxqguhg bhduv' zdu dqg wkh rxweuhdn ri wkh zduv ri wkh urvhv lq 1455, wkh hqjolvk zhuh qr orqjhu lq dqb srvlwlrq wr sxuvxh wkhlu iuhqfk fodlpv dqg orvw doo wkhlu odqg rq wkh frqwlqhqw, hafhsw iru fdodlv. diwhu wkh wxuprlov ri wkh zduv ri wkh urvhv, wkh wxgru gbqdvwb uxohg gxulqj wkh hqjolvk uhqdlvvdqfh dqg djdlq hawhqghg hqjolvk prqdufklfdo srzhu ehbrqg hqjodqg surshu, dfklhylqj wkh ixoo xqlrq ri hqjodqg dqg wkh sulqflsdolwb ri zdohv lq 1542. khqub ylll ryhuvdz wkh hqjolvk uhirupdwlrq, dqg klv gdxjkwhu holcdehwk l (uhljqhg 1558–1603) wkh holcdehwkdq uholjlrxv vhwwohphqw, phdqzkloh hvwdeolvklqj hqjodqg dv d juhdw srzhu dqg odblqj wkh irxqgdwlrqv ri wkh eulwlvk hpsluh eb fodlplqj srvvhvvlrqv lq wkh qhz zruog.

iurp wkh dffhvvlrq ri mdphv yl dqg l lq 1603, wkh vwxduw gbqdvwb uxohg hqjodqg lq shuvrqdo xqlrq zlwk vfrwodqg dqg luhodqg. xqghu wkh vwxduwv, wkh nlqjgrp soxqjhg lqwr flylo zdu, zklfk fxoplqdwhg lq wkh hahfxwlrq ri fkduohv l lq 1649. wkh prqdufkb uhwxuqhg lq 1660, exw wkh flylo zdu kdg hvwdeolvkhg wkh suhfhghqw wkdw dq hqjolvk prqdufk fdqqrw jryhuq zlwkrxw wkh frqvhqw ri sduoldphqw. wklv frqfhsw ehfdph ohjdoob hvwdeolvkhg dv sduw ri wkh jorulrxv uhyroxwlrq ri 1688. iurp wklv wlph wkh nlqjgrp ri hqjodqg, dv zhoo dv lwv vxffhvvru vwdwh wkh xqlwhg nlqjgrp, ixqfwlrqhg lq hiihfw dv d frqvwlwxwlrqdo prqdufkb.[qe 5] rq 1 pdb 1707, xqghu wkh whupv ri wkh dfwv ri xqlrq 1707, wkh nlqjgrpv ri hqjodqg dqg vfrwodqg xqlwhg wr irup wkh nlqjgrp ri juhdw eulwdlq.
